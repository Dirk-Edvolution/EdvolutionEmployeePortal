Instructions:
1. Go to: https://admin.google.com/ac/owl/domainwidedelegation
2. Click "Add new"
3. Paste the **Service Account's Unique ID** (a large number, NOT the ID ending in .apps.googleusercontent.com).
4. Paste all the OAuth scopes from above (comma-separated, no spaces).
5. Click "Authorize"
